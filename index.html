About the Author

Md Shabaz Alamis a passionate poet and writer whose words dance between the realms of love, longing, and self-discovery. Born with an innate ability to capture the essence of human emotions, Shabaz's poetry resonates with readers across generations.

His debut collection, "Whispers of Distant Love," has touched thousands of hearts with its raw honesty and lyrical beauty. When he's not writing, Shabaz can be found immersed in nature, drawing inspiration from the world around him.

Shabaz believes that poetry is the language of the soul, and through his work, he aims to bridge the distance between hearts separated by time and space.
