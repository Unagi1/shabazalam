Whispers of a Distant Love

Fiction/Romance May 20 2025

Whispers of a Distant Love is a poignant tale of two childhood friends, Saahir and Gul, who reunite after a decade apart and fall into a deep long-distance love. Bound by memories but separated by fate, their story explores the beauty of connection, the ache of separation, and a love that lingers—even when goodbye is inevitable.

About The Author

Mohammad Shabaz Alam

Mohammad Shabaz Alam, writing under the pen name md.shabazalam01, is a financial analyst by profession and a storyteller by passion. Residing in Pune, he balances numbers by day and emotions by night—crafting verses, weaving narratives, and capturing silences that often go unheard. His journey as a writer began in 2019, with a love for shayari and short stories rooted in nostalgia, longing, and human connection. Over the years, he has contributed as a co-author to several anthologies, lending his voice to stories that resonate across time and hearts. Whispers of a Distant Love is his debut solo novel—a labor of love that took nearly two years to complete. The book is more than fiction; it is an emotional excavation of love lost, memory held, and the delicate threads that tether souls across years and silences. You can find him sharing his words on Instagram as @md.shabazalam01.
